Bio

Note: homepage work in progress... I'm a fifth year PhD student at BDSC Lab, in UIC, advised by Prof. Philip S. Yu. My reseach interst is Natural Language Processing. I develop generative tools to handle structured data such as Table. I'm also interested in Large Language Models, Semantic Parsing, and Retrieval Language Model . Prior to joining UIC, I received my master degree in Software Engineering from Shandong University with the supervision of Prof. Yilong Yin and Prof. Lijin Wang in 2017. I obtained my bachelor degree from Shandong University in 2014.
